My Grocery List

A simple and efficient way to manage your grocery shopping

About the App

My Grocery List is a user-friendly application that helps you manage your grocery shopping efficiently. Keep track of your shopping items locally on your device with plans for future cloud sync capabilities.

Features

  • Create and manage multiple shopping lists
  • Local storage for privacy
  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • Works offline
